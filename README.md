# Grouping
Welcome to Grouping, an open source platform and, above all, an alternative to guilded and discord

## Why use Grouping?
Grouping is 100% open source, you can see everything that is happening in your application, in addition to being an independent platform created just by me. In addition, it will be a much more moderate platform with a ban on NSFW content and other content.
